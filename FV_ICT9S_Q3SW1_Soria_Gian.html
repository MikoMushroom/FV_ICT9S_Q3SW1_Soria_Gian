𝙈𝙄𝙐 𝙄𝙍𝙐𝙈𝘼

The Ultimate Inventor...
She is a very energetic character who does not think before she speaks. Her mind is filled with creativity and was able to make a few inventions while at this school. She was the type to bully and tease people around and was very playful with people. She was sort of the the typical comic releife. Even tho she is quite a mean person, she was a very big part of the story as she dominated the investigations using her gadgets and logic. She got murdered by one of her inventions as she attempted to murder someone but failed and got her karma.

𝙃𝙄𝙈𝙄𝙆𝙊 𝙔𝙐𝙈𝙀𝙉𝙊

The Ultimate Magician...
Or you may call her by her prefered title, The Ultimate Mage. Himiko is a very sleepy/tired character as she takes so long to respond back in conversations and is always not paying attention. Everyone always suspected her because people had ideas of the "too innocent" person being the actual killer. After many deaths and executions she stayed strong after many of her friends died. Suprisingly, even tho she is considered to be the weakest person in the school out of 16 students she managed to survive with two other people.

𝙏𝙀𝙉𝙆𝙊 𝘾𝙃𝘼𝘽𝘼𝙎𝙃𝙄𝙍𝘼

The Ultimate Neo Aikido Master...
Tenko was a character who had bravery and encouragement, she met Himiko and became her biggest fan as she loved her magic tricks. Tenko was always the shy type, ay kind of compliment she gets she starts to turn red and just either MAYBE kick someone. She sadly got murdered as she was sacrificed along with with Angie Yonaga who got murdered at night because she was walking to her room. Himiko was devistated and swore revenge. In the next chapter after she was murdered, Himiko was able to avenge Tenko

𝙏𝙎𝙐𝙈𝙐𝙂𝙄 𝙎𝙃𝙄𝙍𝙊𝙂𝘼𝙉𝙀

The Ultimate Cosplayer...
Tsumugi isnt really the most noticeable character. She mostly just stands by and speaks when she feels like she need too. She also dosnt have any realations with other characters as she didnt really make friends. All of this has made people think she was just a normal person. In fact, she was the reason why everyone was there, she planned the whole murder mystery and was the actual first person to murder someone and friend another person which caused someone to be eliminated but not her. In the end, she died being crushed by a boulder at a cutscene when the whole school started collapsing.

𝙆𝘼𝙀𝘿𝙀 𝘼𝙆𝘼𝙈𝘼𝙏𝙎𝙐

The Ultimate Pianist...
Kaede is the protagonist of this series. She is just a normal protagonist trying to inspire everyone and encourage everyone. Her character is very interesting because she was a different protagonist. She had a motive to kill the person behind all of this to let everyone free. She killed someone trying to save everyone thinking it was the person behind all of this. She thought she was succesful but in reality she wasnt. She killed Rantaro and was blamed for it, but in reality Tsumugi killed him. She was sadly executed shortly after even tho she wasnt the one who killed him. The info was only revealed in the end of the series.

𝙈𝘼𝙆𝙄 𝙃𝘼𝙍𝙐𝙆𝘼𝙒𝘼

The Ultimate "baby Sitter"...
Maki was a secretive character. She always isolated herself from others and didnt really attend meetings at dinner. Maki was a cold/serious character that only speaks when spoken to. In the middle of the series, it was reveal that she wasnt a baby sitter, ironically she was The Ultimate Assasin. People didnt really believe her at that point and always kept accusing her. She had never killed anyone in the series and was one of the three survivors. She was sort of a detective of the game and was a key character of the whole story. She went insane after her love interest died in the end.

By the end of the story, many deaths and executions have occured. Our protagonist has died and Suichi Saihara was our new protagonist till the end. Rantaro, Ryoma, Tenko with Angie, Miu, and kokichi have been murdered. While on the other hand, Kaede, Kirumi, Korekiyo, Gont, and Kaito were killers and executed after they were caught. Suichi, Himiko, and Maki survived till the very end while Keebo had to sacrifice himself to save everyone and Tsumugi got crushed by a boulder from the collapsing school at the very end of the story.
